Cubical Type Theory

A short course for a type theorist: understand HoTT and cubical well enough to follow proofs and explain the ideas in your own words.

How to read this Each lesson is short and self-contained, with a single tangible win, a primary source to read next, and retrieval questions at the end. Work them in order. Ask your teacher (the agent) anything that is unclear.

Lessons

#LessonThe win
1 Types are Spaces Read "types are ∞-groupoids and equalities are paths" and unpack every word.
2 Why This Matters: The Payoff Explain, on the Bool/Binary example, what univalence buys and what living without it costs.
3 h-levels: How Tall Is the Tower? Name which rung a type sits on, and why the universe of sets is one rung above its sets.
4 The two equalities (coming next) Judgmental vs propositional equality; intensional vs extensional type theory.
